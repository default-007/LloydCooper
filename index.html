About Us

Innovative enterprise
solutions you can trust.

Lloyd Cooper Consulting Group is a leading, independent auditing, tax and accounting firm. We are driven by one mantra — to make an impact that matters in the most sustainable way. To us, that means impact to our clients and their businesses, ideas and actions that look out for the greater good.

We have been in operation for over 10 years, and have built a reputation for delivering high-quality, tailored solutions that help our clients achieve their goals. Our headquarters are in Nairobi, with branches in Uganda, Rwanda, and Somalia. Our team is made up of highly qualified and experienced professionals who work closely with clients to understand their unique needs and challenges.
